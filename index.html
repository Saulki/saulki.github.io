Design process philosophy

Simplicity, attention to details and unnecessary details rejection is a key in a great design. The task is to solve very difficult problem and make it so simple and usual, that everybody would think it was an easy task. User should use the product without giving attention to design. Then we can say the design is successful.

Designers must concentrate on user experience and simplicity, because design is not a visual appearance of product, but the way it works.

Workplace

A place we work at is very important for our creativity. Work space should be unique, warm and meeting place where everyone feels good.

I prefer an office, but I still like periodically work from home. Working with others can be productive, but at the same time it can be distracting if you need focussed concentrated time.

If designer has a freedom to figure out what works best for him, then he will be passionate person who you’ve got to instruct to take a break rather than he that needs supervision. Ofcourse he must love doing what they do, like me.
